# Pocket Vault Theme Refresh Notes

## Direction
- Bright premium financial product.
- Calm, luminous, and layered.
- Softer depth than earlier phases, with clearer separation between canvas, elevated surfaces, and highlighted action states.

## Key Theme Changes
- Backgrounds moved from warm neutral to cooler blue-gray light.
- Surfaces now use brighter white and glass-like tones with softer borders.
- Accent remained blue but became cleaner and slightly more vivid for progress and CTA moments.
- Success and warning colors were tuned to feel system-like rather than loud.

## Surface Hierarchy
- Canvas: pale, luminous page background.
- Elevated surface: primary card plane.
- Floating surface: stronger shadow and glow for hero, success, and artifact cards.
- Muted surface: supportive panels and explanatory notices.

## Typography Direction
- Strengthened heading scale and display hierarchy.
- Kept the system-font approach for cross-platform consistency.
- Increased visual contrast between large amount text, section titles, and supporting copy.

## Shape And Depth
- Larger radii for cards and pill controls.
- Softer shadows with longer spread.
- Thin highlight lines and ambient glows to create depth without visual noise.

## What Was Intentionally Avoided
- Neon gradients and dark crypto aesthetics.
- Heavy blur dependencies.
- Overly glossy or luxury-banking gimmicks.
- Web-only CSS treatments that would diverge from native.
